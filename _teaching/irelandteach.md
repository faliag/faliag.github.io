I served as a Lecturer (Assistant Professor equivalent) in the School of Health Sciences at Atlantic Technological University (ATU), teaching both undergraduate and graduate courses in healthcare management and financial accounting.

### Courses Taught
- **International Healthcare Management (MHSc/Graduate)** — Fall 2019  
- **Management and Financial Accounting (BSc/Undergraduate)** — Fall 2019  
- **International Healthcare Management (MHSc/Graduate, Virtual)** — Spring 2020  
- **Management and Financial Accounting (BSc/Undergraduate; Virtual)** — Spring 2020  

### Course Purpose
These courses provided students with foundational and advanced competencies in global healthcare systems, organizational management, and financial decision‑making within health services organizations.

### Course Overview
The International Healthcare Management course examined global health systems, comparative health policy, leadership, and strategic management in international contexts.  
The Management and Financial Accounting course introduced students to accounting principles, financial reporting, budgeting, and cost analysis relevant to healthcare organizations.

### Teaching Responsibilities
- Delivered lectures in both in‑person and virtual formats  
- Developed course materials, assessments, and case‑based learning activities  
- Taught financial accounting concepts to undergraduate students with diverse backgrounds  
- Guided graduate students through comparative health system analyses  
- Provided detailed feedback on assignments and facilitated group discussions  
- Adapted teaching strategies for virtual instruction during Spring 2020
